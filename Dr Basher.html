Dr. Elbasher Ahmed

He is currently a research fellow in the Materials Research Department at iThemba LABS - Cape Town, South Africa. He obtained a Higher Diploma in Mathematical Sciences at AIMS, South Africa. His MSc degree in Experimental Nuclear Physics from Stellenbosch University (in collaboration with iThemba LABS). He holds a PhD degree in Applied Physics from the University of Cape Town. Working in different research areas makes him a multi-disciplinary researcher. He has been involved in materials science, nuclear physics and mathematical modelling. His activities are based on computational research such as modelling of granular flows, DFT calculations and machine learning. Dr. Ahmed has published several papers in internationally accredited peer-reviewed journals and regularly supervises postgraduate students. Also, he has a long experience of teaching undergrad and postgrad students. Dr Elbasher Ahmed also has been involved in different community- engagement activities such as public talks, teaching short courses and Establishing the Photon Centre
